Understanding the Disease

What is ROP?

Retinopathy of Prematurity is a potentially blinding eye disorder affecting premature infants

Who is Affected?

Infants born prematurely with low birth weight are at highest risk. The shorter the gestation period, the greater the likelihood of developing ROP.

How Does It Develop?

Abnormal blood vessel growth in the retina causes blood leakage and potential damage to the eye structure, leading to vision impairment or blindness.

Why Early Detection?

Early diagnosis is critical. With timely intervention, vision loss can be prevented in most cases. AI enables faster, more accessible screening.
